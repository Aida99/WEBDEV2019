About us

FLOWERCITY.KZ is a flower delivery service that values the trust and respect of its customers. That is why we buy from time-tested suppliers only the highest quality and best plants.

The main feature of our company is that we never leave them in stock for the freshness and attractiveness of flower bouquets.

Our company provides only fresh flowers, so your bouquets will stay at your home in attractive look at least one week.

On our website you can place an order at any time, day or night, after you have chosen the bouquet you like and placed an order, the manager will contact you within 10 minutes.

Delivery usually takes 60-90 minutes.
